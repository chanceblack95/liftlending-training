Objections & rebuttals
"I don't want to close my credit cards."
You've already used up 90% of your available credit. Once we clear these off you'll have a fresh start with new cards. Right now these are basically expensive debit cards trapping you in debt and hurting your credit. These banks will be happy to lend to you again — that's how they make money. The sooner they can get you into new accounts the sooner they can start profiting off you again. We just need to focus on getting you out of debt.
"I don't want my credit score affected."
Your credit is being affected right now. You'll see a dip when accounts close — but once they get cleared, all derogatories get cleared as well. And every payment you make to the program gets reported to the credit bureaus to help you get your credit back in the 700s!
"I don't want to ruin my relationship with these creditors."
These banks will be happy to lend to you again — that's how they make money. The sooner they can get you into new accounts the sooner they can start profiting off you again. We just need to focus on getting you out of debt.
"I've done something like this before and it hurt me."
What you did was traditional debt settlement — where they have you default on all your payments and negotiate with your creditors to settle for a reduced amount. This is completely different. We don't negotiate at all. We legally invalidate the debt and get it completely removed from your report like it was never there in the first place.
"How do I know this isn't a scam?"
A couple things I can clear up: first, we're on a secure recorded line — all calls are recorded for compliance and quality assurance. We work in 45 states across the US. I'm also happy to give you our website so you can do your due diligence. You'll get a full client dashboard once you sign up so you can track everything every step of the way. And we're a licensed company, been in business over 10 years, and A+ rated with the Better Business Bureau.
"I want to think it over."
Ask more questions to find the real concern. Address it directly. Try at least 3 rebuttals before setting a callback. Same-day or next-day callbacks only — never beyond 24 hours. Create urgency: every day they wait is more interest down the drain.
"How do you know creditors will work with you?"
We don't need your creditors to work with us — we work directly with the credit bureaus to clear it up.
Creating urgency on callbacks
"I completely understand — but I want you to think about this: every single day you're paying approximately $[DAILY INTEREST] just in interest to keep up with these accounts. That's money gone forever with nothing to show for it."

"Most people put this off and then call back six months later having paid thousands more in interest — and we're in the exact same spot. The only difference is you're six months deeper."

"I'm not trying to pressure you — I just want to make sure you understand the cost of waiting."
Key mindset reminders
Start your rebuttal with a light laugh / brush-off while you answer — let them feel your smile and confidence through the phone. It eases concerns and shows you already have all the bases covered.
CONFIDENCE IS KEY. If you're unsure of an answer — answer confidently to the best of your knowledge, then offer to put them on a brief hold to get clarification from your supervisor. Never stutter or make it seem like you're making it up.
If you know the client is beyond your comprehension level or too skeptical after your best efforts — don't be scared to let them go. You'd rather not sign someone up than hurt your numbers with a cancel or chargeback.
